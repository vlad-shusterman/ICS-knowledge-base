Flora is a good fairy.   The oldest of the three fairies loves to command and decide, although she recognizes the power of Maleficents .   Flora has dark gray hair. In the guise of a fairy, Flora wears a red-and-red dress with a golden lining. She also wears a dark red cloak with a golden rivet in the shape of a square, a red cap interlaced with a golden ribbon and black shoes.
