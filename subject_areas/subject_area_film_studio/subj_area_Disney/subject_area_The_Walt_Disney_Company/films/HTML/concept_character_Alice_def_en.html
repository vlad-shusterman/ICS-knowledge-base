Alice - the main character of the cartoon "Alice in Wonderland" which is about seven years old. In the cartoon Alice appears as a schoolgirl with a bizarre-logical mindset, whose straight hair "forever climb into the eyes." Alice was described as tender, like a fallow deer, courteous to everyone, trusting and “inquisitive to the extreme, with that taste for life, which is available only to a happy childhood, when everything is new and good, and Sin and Sadness are just words - empty words that mean nothing! ”
