The March Hare is a crazy hare Alice meets at Mad Tea Party. He invites the little girl to drink wine and believes that you should always say what you think. The character also attended the trial of Valet Hearts, where he denied everything. The hare did not appear in the early version of the cartoon. The appearance of the character was influenced by the saying, popular in the days of Carroll - "Insane as the March Hare." The phrase itself comes from observing the “crazy jumps” of hares in March during the mating period.
