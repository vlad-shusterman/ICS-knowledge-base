Lilo and Stich

Lilo is a little girl living in Hawaii. She is an orphan and lives with her older sister Nani. Lilo loves all animals, but most of all she made friends with a dog named Stitch, who is actually not a dog at all, but a disguised alien. It was like this: a criminal named Stitch was transferred to an intergalactic prison, but he managed to escape and land on Earth, where he met Lilo. And here Stich pretended to be a dog in order to escape from the prosecution of the intergalactic police. Now Stich is learning what he hasn't been taught - caring for others
