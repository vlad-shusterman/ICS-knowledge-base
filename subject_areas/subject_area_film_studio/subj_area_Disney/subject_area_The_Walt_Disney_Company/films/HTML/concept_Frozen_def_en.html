Frozen

When the ancient prediction comes true and the kingdom plunges into the arms of eternal winter, three fearless heroes - Princess Anne, the brave Kristoff and his faithful deer Sven - go to the mountains to find Anna's sister, Elsa, who can remove the chilling curse from the country. Along the way, many exciting surprises and exciting adventures await them: a meeting with mystical trolls, an encounter with a charming snowman named Olaf, mountain peaks more abruptly than Everest and magic in every snowflake. Anna and Kristoff have to unite and confront the mighty elements in order to save the kingdom and those who are dear to them ...
