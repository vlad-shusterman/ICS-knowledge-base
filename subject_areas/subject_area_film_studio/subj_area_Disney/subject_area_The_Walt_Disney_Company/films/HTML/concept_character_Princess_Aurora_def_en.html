Princess Aurora is the only daughter of King Stephen and Queen Leah, as a child, a victim of witchcraft by a powerful evil fairy Maleficents . Pissed off at her parents, Maleficent sends a spell on the princess, according to which Princess Aurora should die on the day of his sixteenth birthday, pricking a finger against the spindle spinning wheel. However, the efforts of the three good fairies Flora , Fauna and Marywezes can soften the curse by replacing death with a deep magic dream, from which the “sleeping beauty” can be awakened only with the help of a true love kiss.
