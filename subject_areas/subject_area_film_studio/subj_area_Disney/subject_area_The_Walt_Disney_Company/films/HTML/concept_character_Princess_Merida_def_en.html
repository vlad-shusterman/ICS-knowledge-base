Princess Merida - the main character of the cartoon "Braveheart" . The only daughter of King Fergus and Queen Elionor . Princess Merida , like a real princess, should be an example to everyone.  Her life is arranged by the minute: mother watches her every step, and rarely comes the day when the young princess is left to herself and can do that  what she likes is riding a horse, shooting a bow and climbing steep rocks. Merida's Father  does not see anything wrong with his daughter's hobbies, but does not dare to cross to wife .
