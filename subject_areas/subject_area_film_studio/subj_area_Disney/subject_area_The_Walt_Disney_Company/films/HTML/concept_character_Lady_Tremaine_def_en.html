Lady Tremayne - A slender elderly lady with a white stripe in her hair.  She has gray eyes, but when she wants to do evil, a green sheen appears in them.  Father Cinderella after the death of his wife married a second time, Lady Tremaine < >.  She already had two daughters, Anastasia Tremaine and   Drizela Tremaine , as well as the cat Lucifer.  After the death of her husband, Lady Tremaine showed her true face -  she strongly oppressed and humiliated her stepdaughter Cinderella ,  forcing her to do all the dirty housework.
