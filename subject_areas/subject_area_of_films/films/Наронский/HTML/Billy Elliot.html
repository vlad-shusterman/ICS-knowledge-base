What should a miner's son get involved in? No doubt: boxing and boxing only. But 11-year-old Billy Elliot had his own opinion on this. He was in love with ... ballet. Tony's father and older brother go into a rage when they find out that he had “exchanged boxing” for a less “courageous ballet”. And only the sharp-tongued Mrs. Wilkinson supports Billy in his desire to dance, and he gets a chance to enter the Royal Ballet School.
