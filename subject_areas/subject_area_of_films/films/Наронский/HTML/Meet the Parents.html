The owner of a wonderful family, the orderly Greg Tracher, goes to the country to meet the parents of his girlfriend Pam. Here, Greg is going to solemnly ask for the hand of his beloved. But, unfortunately, Greg is an unlucky guy, and any good initiatives in his performance turn into a nightmare. Unhappy Tracher and in the most terrible dream did not imagine what tests await him. The fact is that Pam's father, Jack Burns, a retired CIA employee, arranges his special and tough test for his daughter's friends. This test even includes a special lie detector test!
